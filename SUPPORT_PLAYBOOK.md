## 4. Replacement / Support Boundary

### What IS Covered (30-day warranty)
- Account stops working through no fault of the customer
- Login credentials fail
- Workspace invite expires
- Technical issues on our side

### What is NOT Covered
- Customer changes password on shared account
- Customer uses multiple devices simultaneously on shared plan
- Customer violates tool provider's terms
- Customer shares credentials with others
- Account banned due to misuse

### Replacement Script
```
I understand your [product] isn't working. Let me check.

If the issue is on our side, I'll replace it within 24 hours at no extra cost.

Please confirm:
1. You haven't changed the password
2. You haven't shared the credentials
3. You used only 1 device at a time (shared plans)

Once confirmed, I'll send new access immediately.
```

## 6. What NOT to Promise

❌ Do NOT claim:
- "Official partner of OpenAI/Google/Anthropic"
- "Guaranteed account safety forever"
- "Unlimited usage" (use "higher limits" or "generous limits")
- "#1 in Bangladesh" (use "trusted local provider")
- "Instant delivery" (use "fast delivery: 5–15 min")
- "Lifetime access"
- Fake review counts or ratings

✅ DO say:
- "Genuine subscriptions sourced through legitimate channels"
- "30-day replacement warranty under our policy"
- "Fast delivery: 5–15 minutes for most plans"
- "Local payment support: bKash, Nagad, Bank Transfer"
- "Bangla + English WhatsApp support"
- "Not affiliated with third-party AI providers"

## 7. Escalation Rules

| Issue | First Response | Escalate If |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| General inquiry | Support playbook script | Customer asks for owner/manager |
| Payment problem | Verify transaction ID | Payment not received after 24hr |
| Access not working | Replacement within 24hr | Multiple replacements needed |
| Account banned | Explain policy, no replacement | Customer disputes policy |
| Refund request | Explain policy, offer credit | Customer threatens legal action |
| Technical bug | Report to dev team | Affects multiple customers |

## 8. Support Closure Flow

1. Confirm issue is resolved
2. Ask for confirmation: "Is everything working now?"
3. Offer additional help: "Anything else I can help with?"
4. Close positively: "Thanks for choosing AI Team Premium BD! 🙏"
